Tips collected by people from groups

- Do NOT JUMP of the boat party or you will be permanently banned from this years festival (all days).

- No drinks or food allowed to be taken on site.

- Come at least 20 mins before boat party departure to not get left out of it.

- There ARE lockers available at the site.

- There IS a FREE shuttle to the Barbarellas and back.

- Have fun!
